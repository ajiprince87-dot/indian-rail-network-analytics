# Indian Rail Network Analytics

A Data-Driven Power BI Dashboard analyzing Indian Railways Route Efficiency & Distance Performance using Open Government Data.

## Key Project Insights
* **Total Train Volume:** The network manages a massive total of 186.12K trains, indicating highly dense railway operations.
* **Average Travel Distance:** The average distance covered stands at 281.60 km, showing a high concentration of short to medium-route services.
* **Busiest Hubs:** CSMT, TNA, and KYN handle the maximum number of train stops, with CSMT leading as the primary central terminal.
* **Top Destinations:** The donut chart reveals HWH (32.38%) and SDAH (29.18%) as the major destination hubs.
* **Peak Traffic Hours:** A significant spike in train movement is observed right after midnight (00:00 AM) due to overnight long-distance and freight operations.

## Business Recommendations
* **Infrastructure Expansion:** Add extra platforms at high-traffic hubs like CSMT and KYN to minimize delays.
* **Resource Optimization:** Deploy additional staff during midnight peak hours to manage train scheduling and ground operations efficiently.
* **Maintenance Scheduling:** Utilize low-traffic daytime windows for track maintenance to ensure uninterrupted night services.
